<p>We take your data privacy serious. It is our highest priority to offer a privacy-first solution, both self-hosted and in our managed cloud. We take on considerable additional effort to collect as little data as neccessary and handle it safely and securely.</p>

<h3>Important Notice</h3>
<p>This Privacy Policy only applies to the Formbricks Cloud under <a href="https://app.formbricks.com">app.formbricks.com</a> (‚ÄùCloud‚Äù) and our Landing Page under formbricks.com. (‚ÄùLanding Page‚Äù) If you are self-hosting our open source tool on your own servers, this Privacy Policy does not apply.</p>
<h3>Definitions</h3>
<p>To avoid confusion, let‚Äôs define who we collect data from:</p>
<ul>
<li>Researcher: the person who uses the Cloud to create and distribute Formbricks forms and surveys or manage submissions from other forms</li>
<li>Website visitors: People who visit our marketing landing page</li>
</ul>
<h3>We are data processor, you are data controller</h3>
<p>With our Cloud we are a provider of this Service. We are not the owner of the collected personal data from survey respondents. The Researcher (the person who sends out a form i.e. you) is responsible for the data they collect and thus data controller of respondent data.</p>

<p>This is the information we collect from <strong>Website visitors</strong> and <strong>Researchers</strong> as long as you are using our Service:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Electronical identification data</strong>: including anonymized IP address, device &amp; browser data;</li>
<li><strong>Anonymized information about your use of our website</strong>: including how you end up on our website, what actions you perform and the pages you visited.</li>
<li><strong>Registration information:</strong>

<li><strong>Billing information:</strong>
If you pay for Formbricks, we will ask for your billing details including name, address and financial information depending on your payment method (stored by our payment service provider Stripe)</li>
<li><strong>Survey data:</strong>
We store your survey data (questions and responses) for you and provide tools for you to analyse and use this data.</li>
<li><strong>Error logs:</strong>
We collect error logs to help us diagnose and fix issues with our service.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="scroll-mt-24" id="how-we-use-your-data"><strong>How we use your data</strong></h2>
<p>Formbricks does not sell personal data to third parties. Generally, we only collect your data with your consent in order to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide and improve our Service.</li>
<li>Email you essential updates and offers (only if you&#x27;re interested).</li>
<li>Ensure our product works smoothly and fix any issues.</li>
<li>Respond to legal situations and prevent fraud.</li>
<li>Let you engage with our Service&#x27;s interactive features.</li>
<li>Support you when you need help.</li>
<li>Track how our Service is used.</li>
<li>Handle technical problems.</li>
<li>Manage our relationship with you, including billing.</li>
<li>Inform you about account changes or renewals.</li>
<li>Share news and offers about our other services (only if you&#x27;ve shown interest).</li>
<li>Use data in ways we explain when you provide it.</li>
<li>Other reasons, but only with your agreement.</li>
</ul>

<p>We will also retain Usage Data for internal analysis purposes. <h3>What types of cookies does Formbricks use?</h3>
<p><strong>Required cookies:</strong> Certain cookies are necessary in order for the website to operate correctly and remain secure. For example, we use cookies to authenticate you. When you log on to our website, authentication cookies are set which let us know who you are during a browsing session. We have to load essential cookies for legitimate interests pursued by us in delivering our Sites essential functionality to you.</p>
<h3>How can you block or eliminate cookies?</h3>
<p>We only use cookies when you are logged into our Service. You can allow, eliminate, or block cookies within your browser. If you block all cookies, some websites will not work properly anymore.</p>
