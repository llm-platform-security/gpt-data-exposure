<h2>About</h2>
<p>
<i>icanhazdadjoke.com</i> is the internet's largest selection of dad jokes.
</p>
<p>
This site was written and is maintained by <a href="https://brett.is/">Brett Langdon</a>.
</p>

<h2>Slack integration</h2>
<p>
By adding the <i>icanhazdadjoke</i> Slack app to your team, you will be adding a <code>/dadjoke</code> slash command which will provide you with a random joke from <i>icanhazdadjoke.com</i>.
</p>
<p>
This app will only add a slash command to your team. We do not store any information about the Slack teams that integrate with this app, and we do not track or store any messages from that Slack team. We only respond to the request for </code>/dadjoke</code>.
</p>
<h2>HipChat integration</h2>
<p>
By adding the <i>icanhazdadjoke</i> HipChat app to your team, you will be adding a <code>/dadjoke</code> slash command which will provide you with a random joke from <i>icanhazdadjoke.com</i>.
</p>
<p>
This app will only add a slash command to your team. We do not store any information about the HipChat teams that integrate with this app, and we do not track or store any messages from that HipChat team. We only respond to messages that start with </code>/dadjoke</code>.
</p>
<h2>Twist integration</h2>
<p>
Adding the <i>icanhazdadjoke</i> Twist integration to your team, will provide you with a <code>/dadjoke <term></code> slash command that you can use to fetch a dad joke for a specific topic.
</p>
<p>
<i>Note:</i> You must provide <code>/dadjoke</code> with a search term, e.g. <code>/dadjoke movie</code>.
</p>
