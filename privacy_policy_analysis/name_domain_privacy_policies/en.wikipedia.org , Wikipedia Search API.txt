<div class="wp-block-shiro-landing-page-hero hero is-style-purple"><header class="hero__header"><div class="hero__text-column"><small class="hero__kicker">Advocacy</small><h1 class="hero__title">We protect the right to free knowledge.</h1></div><figure class="hero__image-container image-filter-inherit"><img decoding="async" alt="" class="hero__image" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Editatona_Escuela_de_mujeres_wikipedistas_02.jpg?w=1200&amp;h=675&amp;crop=1"/></figure></header><div class="hero__intro"><p>Everyone, everywhere has the right to access and share knowledge. By working with Wikimedia volunteers and affiliate organizations around the world, we protect free and open knowledge online, the people who make it possible, and our shared values.</p></div></div>



<div class="wp-block-shiro-stairs">
<div class="wp-block-shiro-stair stair"><h2 class="stair__heading is-style-h3">Defending free expression</h2><img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" class="wp-image-61192 size-image_16x9_small stair__image" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Transparency_Report_1-e1565971921744.jpg?w=600&amp;h=338&amp;crop=1" height="338" width="600"/><p class="stair__body">We defend everyone’s right to access free knowledge. We protect free expression rights; oppose government-imposed <a href="https://wikimediafoundation.org/news/2023/02/03/wikimedia-foundation-urges-pakistan-telecommunications-authority-to-restore-access-to-wikipedia-in-pakistan/">censorship</a>; and educate governments, regulators, and lawmakers to defend <a href="https://diff.wikimedia.org/2022/04/21/how-wikimedia-and-partner-organizations-helped-ensure-us-companies-continue-to-support-access-to-free-knowledge-for-the-russian-people/">people’s right</a> to access and share knowledge globally.</p></div>



<div class="wp-block-shiro-stair stair"><h2 class="stair__heading is-style-h3">Advancing better internet regulations</h2><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-57346 size-image_16x9_small stair__image" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/Felix_Satdeep_Stalinjeet_and_Manavpreet_at_Wikimedia_Conference_2017-e1532738694295.jpg?w=600&amp;h=338&amp;crop=1" height="338" width="600"/><p class="stair__body">We help lawmakers support the right of online platforms and communities to create and share knowledge. We advocate laws that <a href="https://medium.com/wikimedia-policy/gonzalez-vs-google-whats-at-stake-for-wikipedia-563162797553?source=friends_link&amp;sk=b587ea9afd38932649d4f7312208c3c2">protect our ability to host content</a> that Wikimedia volunteers share and edit. <a href="https://wikimediafoundation.org/about/transparency/current/">We are transparent</a> about how we handle government demands to remove content or share data.</p></div>



<div class="wp-block-shiro-stair stair"><h2 class="stair__heading is-style-h3">Promoting open copyright licenses</h2><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-71328 size-image_16x9_small stair__image" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Collective_photo_at_the_-VisibleWikiWomen_photo_booth.jpg?w=600&amp;h=338&amp;crop=1" height="338" width="600" srcset="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Collective_photo_at_the_-VisibleWikiWomen_photo_booth.jpg?resize=1200,675 1200w, https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Collective_photo_at_the_-VisibleWikiWomen_photo_booth.jpg?resize=600,338 600w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /><p class="stair__body">Creating and remixing content is vital for Wikimedia projects. We support copyright laws that promote a strong public domain and enable the use of <a href="https://diff.wikimedia.org/2021/07/19/statistics-sweden-adopts-cc0-license-for-open-data">free</a> and <a href="https://wikimediafoundation.org/news/2020/10/22/world-health-organization-and-wikimedia-foundation-expand-access/">open</a> licenses so Wikimedia volunteers can contribute to knowledge broadly.</p></div>



<div class="wp-block-shiro-stair stair"><h2 class="stair__heading is-style-h3">Upholding human rights</h2><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-71329 size-image_16x9_small stair__image" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Suscol_Intertribal_Council_2015_Pow-wow_-_Stierch_23.jpg?w=600&amp;h=338&amp;crop=1" height="338" width="600" srcset="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Suscol_Intertribal_Council_2015_Pow-wow_-_Stierch_23.jpg?resize=1200,675 1200w, https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Suscol_Intertribal_Council_2015_Pow-wow_-_Stierch_23.jpg?resize=600,338 600w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /><p class="stair__body">Volunteers may face surveillance, harassment, and retaliation for their work on Wikimedia projects. We defend their <a href="https://diff.wikimedia.org/2021/12/09/what-the-wikimedia-foundations-new-human-rights-policy-means-for-our-movement/">human rights</a>, and we work to promote a <a href="https://medium.com/wikimedia-policy/bills-that-claim-to-advance-safety-online-will-cause-more-online-harm-its-time-to-pay-attention-48746b6dc7e7?source=friends_link&amp;sk=7fdfd8900cc36d41bf353dd26c957de2">safe online environment</a> to create and share knowledge.</p></div>



<div class="wp-block-shiro-stair stair"><h2 class="stair__heading is-style-h3">Protecting privacy</h2><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-61193 size-image_16x9_small stair__image" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Albert_V_Bryan_Federal_District_Courthouse_-_Alexandria_Va-e1565971808499.jpg?w=600&amp;h=338&amp;crop=1" height="338" width="600"/><p class="stair__body">Privacy enables people to engage and share knowledge freely, without fear of someone watching their online lives. We defend everyone’s right to <a href="https://medium.com/wikimedia-policy/wikimedia-foundation-tells-ftc-to-rein-in-commercial-surveillance-e2ef85bddb34?source=friends_link&amp;sk=59f499c4db688b2b4c96615f1fc7f902">privacy</a> and <a href="https://wikimediafoundation.org/news/2023/02/21/u-s-supreme-court-declines-to-hear-wikimedia-foundations-challenge-to-nsa-mass-surveillance/">challenge mass surveillance</a>.</p></div>



<div class="wp-block-shiro-stair stair"><h2 class="stair__heading is-style-h3">Fighting disinformation</h2><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-71330 size-image_16x9_small stair__image" src="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Capacitacion_con_Wikimedia_Argentina_-_2.jpg?w=600&amp;h=338&amp;crop=1" height="338" width="600" srcset="https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Capacitacion_con_Wikimedia_Argentina_-_2.jpg?resize=1200,675 1200w, https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Capacitacion_con_Wikimedia_Argentina_-_2.jpg?resize=600,338 600w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /><p class="stair__body">We stand for the right of everyone, everywhere to participate in free knowledge. In early 2022, when sanctions were imposed against the Russian government for invading Ukraine, we worked with our partners to&nbsp;<a href="https://diff.wikimedia.org/2022/04/21/how-wikimedia-and-partner-organizations-helped-ensure-us-companies-continue-to-support-access-to-free-knowledge-for-the-russian-people/">make sure the Russian people could continue to access the open internet</a>.</p></div>
