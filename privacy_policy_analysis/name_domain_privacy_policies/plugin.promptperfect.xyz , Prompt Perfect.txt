              <div class="faq-answer">
                  <p>Detailed prompts help in narrowing down the potential responses and increase the relevance of the information provided by ChatGPT.</p>
                  <p>Providing structure in the prompts helps in organizing the conversation flow, ensuring that the AI can follow up with appropriate and coherent responses.</p>
                  <p>Adding context helps ChatGPT to discern the user's intent, especially in complex or ambiguous scenarios, leading to a more accurate and satisfying user experience.</p>
                  <p>Most users try to use ChatGPT like Google or a search engine. However, the best results come from context and detail-filled prompts. Prompt Perfect allows you to type simple, search-like prompts and get answers optimized for AI.</p>
                  <p>*Fun Fact* Prompt Perfect also corrects your grammar to ensure ChatGPT knows exactly how to respond.</p>
