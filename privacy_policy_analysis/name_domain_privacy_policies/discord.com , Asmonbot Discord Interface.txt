    <div class="entry-content wp-block-post-content is-layout-constrained wp-block-post-content-is-layout-constrained"><p><strong>Privacy Policy for ONX RP Alerts Extension for Google Chrome</strong></p>
<p><em>Last Updated: 12-11-2023</em></p>
<p>Welcome to the ONX RP Alerts Extension for Google Chrome (“the Extension”). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a transparent understanding of how the Extension operates. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the use of certain permissions and functionalities while emphasizing our commitment to not collecting or storing any of your data.</p>
<p><strong>1. Overview</strong></p>
<p>The ONX RP Alerts Extension is designed to enhance your role-playing experience on various platforms by providing timely notifications, alerts, and relevant content directly through your Google Chrome browser. To ensure optimal functionality, the Extension requires specific permissions which are solely used for operational purposes as detailed below.</p>
<p><strong>2. Permissions and Their Necessities</strong></p>
<p>a. <strong>Storage:</strong> The Extension utilizes local storage on your device to temporarily save your preferences and settings. This allows for a personalized experience and ensures that your custom settings are retained each time you use the Extension. Importantly, this data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted or stored externally.</p>
<p>b. <strong>Notifications:</strong> To deliver real-time alerts and updates, the Extension requests permission to send notifications. These notifications are integral to the core functionality of the Extension, keeping you informed of relevant events and activities without any delay.</p>
<p>c. <strong>Alarms:</strong> The alarms permission is used to schedule regular checks for new updates or content. This ensures that the Extension can alert you promptly as soon as new information becomes available, enhancing your experience without the need for manual refreshes.</p>
<p>d. <strong>Host Permissions:</strong> The Extension requires host permissions to interact with specific websites and services that are essential for delivering the relevant content and alerts. This permission allows the Extension to seamlessly integrate with these services, providing a cohesive and efficient user experience.</p>
<p><strong>3. No Data Collection or Storage</strong></p>
<p>We firmly believe in respecting your privacy and autonomy. To this end, the ONX RP Alerts Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing history. All functionalities are designed to operate without the need to gather or store any user information externally. Your use of the Extension remains private and secure.</p>
<p><strong>4. Data Security</strong></p>
<p>As the Extension does not collect or store any personal data, there are no risks of data breaches or unauthorized access to personal information. Your privacy and security are inherently preserved through our no-data-collection policy.</p>
<p><strong>5. Changes to the Privacy Policy</strong></p>
<p>We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to periodically review this policy to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.</p>
<p><strong>6. Contact Us</strong></p>
<p>If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or your interactions with the Extension, please contact us at snlabat@gmail.com.</p>
<p>By using the ONX RP Alerts Extension, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.</p>
