  <h1>Welcome</h1>




  
            <h2>Welcome</h2>

<p>The&nbsp;University Privacy team provides guidance and resources&nbsp;to help navigate Cornell's regulatory obligations and <a data-entity-substitution="canonical" data-entity-type="node" data-entity-uuid="74852e0b-5a2a-4298-9fea-fff637ad8289" href="/privacy-cornell/privacy-principles-cornell" title="Privacy Principles at Cornell">ethical principles</a> pertaining to the curation and <a data-entity-substitution="canonical" data-entity-type="node" data-entity-uuid="f55ac6c3-2c77-4425-b3be-20ce5cbce9b1" href="/information-use-cornell" title="Information Use at Cornell">use of data</a>.&nbsp;Our team is focused on upholding Cornell's core values, facilitating solutions, enhancing a culture of compliance and ethics, and balancing compliance with mission-driven activities. We work closely with <a href="https://it.cornell.edu">Cornell Information Technology</a>&nbsp;to realize these goals in collaboration with units, colleges, and departments across campus.</p>

<p>University Privacy staff are dedicated to supporting Cornell's&nbsp;<a href="https://www.cornell.edu/about/mission.cfm">mission</a>&nbsp;of learning, discovery,&nbsp;and engagement.&nbsp;</p>
