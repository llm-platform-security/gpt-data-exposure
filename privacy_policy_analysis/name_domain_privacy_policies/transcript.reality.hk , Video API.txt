Welcome to Video Insights GPT (Insights). This Privacy Policy outlines our commitments and your rights regarding the collection, use, and protection of your personal information. It applies to all services and products offered by Insights.

We collect information necessary to provide our services, such as queries and interaction data with our ChatGPT assistant.
