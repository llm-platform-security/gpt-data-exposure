<b>PRIVACY POLICY</b><BR>
<BR>
WBS Publishing, LLC dba The Quote Garden respects the privacy of the visitors of its Website, the home page of which is located at https://www.quotegarden.com/ (the “Website”) and we strive to provide the safest possible environment for our visitors. Please read this Privacy Policy (“Privacy Policy” or “Policy”) carefully, because by visiting our Website, you are bound by the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy. You agree that by accessing our Website you expressly and voluntarily consent to our use and disclosure of the information that you submit.<BR>
<BR>

<b>Overview</b><BR>
<BR>
The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to inform you of our policies and practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of any personal information you submit to us through our Website and to demonstrate our commitment to protecting the information that you submit. This Privacy Policy applies only to information that you have submitted through our Website and does not apply to any other information or Website. Please be advised that this Policy may be amended at any time without notice and it is your responsibility to read it carefully and review any changes that may be made.<BR>
<BR>

<b>Your Information:&#160; Email Communications, Information Collected</b><BR>
<BR>
Presently, no personal information from visitors is gathered or tracked by our Website. The only personal information we see from visitors occurs when visitors send emails to The Quote Garden. No personal information is exchanged through the Website if you attempt to contact and email us. We do not share private email information (names, email addresses, email content) sent through the act of emailing with outside parties. However, please note that we have no control or responsibility over the privacy policies and security of personal email accounts and email service providers.<BR>
<BR>
The Quote Garden does not provide or sell personal identifiable information to other parties. We do not see any personal identifiable information when you visit the Website.<BR> 
<BR>
While we do not track our Website traffic, we do, however, reserve the right to receive your IP address when accessing the Website for security and general site traffic monitoring.<BR>
<BR>

<b>Use of Cookies</b><BR>
<BR>
A cookie is a piece of data stored on a site visitor’s hard drive to help improve access to websites. Beginning <nobr>August 31,</nobr> 2020, this Website is not utilizing tracking cookies. Usage of other cookies is in no way linked to any personal information. Cookies placed by our Website do not store any personal or sensitive data.<BR>
<BR>
Presently, all cookies that may be placed from this Website are exempt from General Data Protection Regulation Act (GDPR) Cookie Consent, as they are necessary for User-Centric Security or User Input.<BR>
<BR>
You may refuse to accept cookies by altering the settings on your internet browser through the “Help” feature on your browser, or for additional assistance, you can click on the “Manage Cookies” section on the Interactive Advertising Bureau website:&#8198; https://www.allaboutcookies.org. Please note that if you adjust your internet browser settings to refuse cookies, it may affect your functionality of this Website and you may be unable to access some or all parts of this Website.<BR>
<BR>
If you visited this Website prior to <nobr>August 31,</nobr> 2020, you may have advertising or analytics cookies in your browser that were stored during previous visits (for example, “__gads” or “__utma” or “__utmz” cookies). You can remove any previously set cookies by following the instructions at the following link:&#8198; https://www.allaboutcookies.org/<BR>
<BR>
Our website search service, Site Search 360, sets a strictly necessary <nobr>“__cfduid”</nobr> cookie for their CDN Cloudfare’s security features and this cannot be turned off; it does not store any personally identifiable information. The <nobr>“__cfduid”</nobr> cookie is exempt from GDPR Cookie Consent as it is required for user-centric security; it is stored for <nobr>1 year</nobr>. Site Search 360 also sets a “ss360LastQuery” cookie to store the last-entered query so that when you navigate back to the search results page, the correct results are shown. The “ss360LastQuery” cookie is also exempt from GDPR Cookie Consent as it falls under User Input category; it is stored for 24 hours.<BR>
<BR>

<BR>
Third-party companies and services may use their own technology to collect information about you for analytics, statistical, and other informational purposes. The information received by these third-party companies and their business partners is subject to the privacy policies of these third-party companies and services. We have no control over the use of such technology and activity, nor are we responsible for such use.<BR>
<BR>
When you perform a search on this Website, your search query is sent to the Site Search 360 server to provide search analytics; this information is aggregated and is not personally identifiable. Your IP address is logged to Site Search 360 to prevent spam and abuse techniques. All communication between you and the search server is encrypted and secured via SSL (Secure Sockets Layer). You can view the Site Search 360 privacy policy through the following link: https://www.sitesearch360.com/privacy-policy/<BR>

Please check the terms of use and privacy policy of any linked websites you visit to find out what data they are collecting and the purposes for which they process your personal information and data.<BR>
