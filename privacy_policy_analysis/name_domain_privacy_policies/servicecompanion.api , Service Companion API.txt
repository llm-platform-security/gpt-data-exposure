      <h1 id="privacy-policy-for-service-companion">Privacy Policy for Service Companion</h1>

<h2 id="introduction">Introduction</h2>
<p>Welcome to Service Companion! We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and sharing of your information when you use our services.</p>

<h2 id="data-collection">Data Collection</h2>
<p>We collect information necessary to provide and enhance our services. This may include:</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Personal Identification Information:</strong> Name, email address, and contact details.</li>
  <li><strong>Transactional Information:</strong> Details of the transactions you carry out through our service.</li>
  <li><strong>Usage Data:</strong> Information on how you use the service, such as your search queries.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="data-use">Data Use</h2>
<p>The information we collect is used for the following purposes:</p>

<ul>
  <li>To facilitate and improve our services.</li>
  <li>To manage your account and transactions.</li>
  <li>To communicate with you, for example, on updates or support issues.</li>
  <li>To conduct research and analysis to improve user experience.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="data-sharing-and-disclosure">Data Sharing and Disclosure</h2>
<p>We do not sell or rent user information. We only share data:</p>

<ul>
  <li>With service providers performing services on our behalf.</li>
  <li>When required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process.</li>
  <li>To protect our rights, property, or safety, and that of our users or the public.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="data-protection">Data Protection</h2>
<p>We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard safeguards designed to prevent unauthorized access and misuse of user information.</p>

<h2 id="user-rights">User Rights</h2>
<p>Users have the right to:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Access, correct, or delete their personal data.</li>
  <li>Object to processing and request restriction of their personal data use.</li>
  <li>Withdraw consent at any time where we rely on consent to process personal data.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="data-retention">Data Retention</h2>
<p>We will retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or to comply with legal obligations.</p>

<h2 id="changes-to-this-policy">Changes to This Policy</h2>
<p>We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our information practices. We will notify you by posting an announcement on the Service Companion website or through the service itself.</p>

<h2 id="contact-us">Contact Us</h2>
<p>If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Email:</strong> <a href="mailto:contact@yoseph.dev">contact@yoseph.dev</a></li>
</ul>
