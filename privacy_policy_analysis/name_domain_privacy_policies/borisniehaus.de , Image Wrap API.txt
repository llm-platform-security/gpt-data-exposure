Privacy Policy for LinkWrap2023

1. Introduction
LinkWrap2023 respects the privacy of its users and is committed to protecting their personal data. This privacy policy outlines how we handle information collected during the use of our service.

2. Data Collection and Use
When you use our service to generate an image, we collect the following information:

The text you enter, which appears on the image.
A unique ID created for each generated image.
This data is used to create the requested image and to provide you with a link to download the image.

3. Data Storage
The generated images are temporarily stored on our server (germany). They are deleted after a set period for privacy reasons.

4. Data Sharing
LinkWrap2023 does not share personal data with third parties, unless required by law. 
The option by OpenAI: "Use conversation data in your GPT to improve our models", is turned off.

5. Data Security
We take appropriate measures to ensure the security of your data. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
