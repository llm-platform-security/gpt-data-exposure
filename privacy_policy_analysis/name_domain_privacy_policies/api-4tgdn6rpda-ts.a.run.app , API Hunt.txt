<h2>Introduction</h2>
<p>Welcome to ApiHunt. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, process, and protect the personal information of our users. Our commitment is to safeguard your privacy and ensure that your personal data is handled responsibly.</p>
<h2>Scope and Consent</h2>
<p>By using ApiHunt.io, you consent to the collection, use, and sharing of your personal data as outlined in this policy. Our services are global, and your data may be processed anywhere we or our service providers operate.</p>
<h2>Information Collection</h2>
<h3>Personal Information</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>For Newsletter Subscriptions:</strong> We collect email addresses for sending newsletters and updates.</li>
<li><strong>Voluntary Submission:</strong> Information you voluntarily provide when contacting us or participating in surveys and promotions.</li>
<li><strong>Account Information:</strong> If we offer account creation, personal details necessary for account setup will be collected.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Non-Personal Information</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Cookies and Tracking Technologies:</strong> We use cookies and similar technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information.</li>
<li><strong>Analytics Data:</strong> We collect data regarding your interaction with our website, preferences, and other analytical data to enhance our services.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Sensitive Information</h3>
<p>We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information and advise users not to share such information with us.</p>
<h2>Use of Collected Information</h2>
<p>The information collected is used for the following purposes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Providing, maintaining, and improving our services.</li>
<li>Communicating with you, including sending newsletters.</li>
<li>Enhancing user experience and website functionality.</li>
<li>Conducting research and analysis for business development.</li>
</ul>
<h2>Data Sharing and Disclosure</h2>
<h3>Third-Party Service Providers</h3>
<p>We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf.</p>
<h3>Legal Compliance and Protection</h3>
<p>We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe that such action is necessary to comply with legal processes, protect our rights, or ensure users&#x27; safety.</p>
<h3>International Transfers</h3>
<p>Your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries other than your own.</p>
<h2>Data Security</h2>
<p>We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.</p>
<h2>Your Rights and Choices</h2>
<h3>Access and Correction</h3>
<p>You have the right to access and correct your personal information.</p>
<h3>Opt-Out</h3>
<p>You may opt-out of receiving newsletters and promotional communications.</p>
<h3>Data Portability</h3>
<p>Where applicable, you may request data portability in compliance with privacy regulations.</p>
<h2>Children’s Privacy</h2>
<p>Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.</p>
<h2>Compliance with Global Regulations</h2>
<p>We endeavor to comply with global data protection regulations, including GDPR for users in the European Union, and other applicable laws.</p>
<h2>Changes to This Policy</h2>
<p>We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. We encourage users to review it periodically for updates.</p>
