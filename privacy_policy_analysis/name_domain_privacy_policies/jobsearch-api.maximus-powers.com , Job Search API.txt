    <h3>Privacy Policy for Enginuity Job Search API</h3>
    Last Updated: Janurary 13, 2024
    
    <h3>Introduction</h3>
    Enginuity Job Search API ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who use our services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our API. We reserve the right to make changes to this policy at any time and for any reason.
    
    <h3>Information Collection</h3>
    Our API collects information in the following ways:
    
    Uploaded Documents: When users upload documents (resumes) to our '/search_by_resume_text' endpoint, we only recieve the text of the document, and not the document itself. We do not store the text of the document after processing.
    
    Form Data: We collect form data submitted to our '/search_by_category_location' endpoint, including category and location information.
    
    Server Logs: Our servers automatically collect information when our API is accessed, including IP addresses, browser types, and access times.
    
    <h3>Use of Information</h3>
    The information collected through our API is used for the following purposes:
    
    To Provide Services: To match resumes with job listings and respond to user queries.
    
    For Improvement: To enhance the functionality and performance of our services.
    
    For Security: To protect our services from unauthorized access and misuse.
    
    <h3>Data Storage and Security</h3>
    We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, no electronic storage method or data transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
    
    <h3>Data Retention</h3>
    Uploaded documents are deleted after processing. Server logs and other data are retained as long as necessary for the purposes described above, in compliance with our data retention policies.
    
    <h3>Data Sharing</h3>
    We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties, except as required by law.
    
    <h3>Children's Privacy</h3>
    Our API is not intended for individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
    
    <h3>Changes to Our Privacy Policy</h3>
    We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.
