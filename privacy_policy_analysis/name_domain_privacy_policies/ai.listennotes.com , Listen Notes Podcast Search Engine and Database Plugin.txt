from figuring out basic stuff like which language you speak,
to more complex things like which ads you’ll find most useful.</p>
<p>We collect information that we get from your use of our services.
We collect information about the services that you use and how you use them.
This information includes:</p>
<ul class="list-disc list-inside">

with your account.</li>
<li>Device information. We collect device-specific information
(such as your hardware model, operating system version, unique device identifiers,
and mobile network information including phone number). </li>
<li>Log information. When you use our services or view content provided by Listen Notes,
we automatically collect and store certain information in server logs. This includes:
<ul class="list-disc list-inside ml-4">
<li>details of how you used our service, such as your search queries.</li>
<li>Internet protocol address.</li>
<li>browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request and referral URL.</li>
<li>cookies that may uniquely identify your browser.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Location information. When you use Listen Notes services, we may collect and process information about your actual location.</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="ln-l2-text pb-4">How we use information we collect</h2>
<p>We use the information we collect from all of our services to provide, maintain, protect and improve them, to develop new ones,
and to protect Listen Notes and our users.
We also use this information to offer you tailored content –
like giving you more relevant search results and ads.
<br><br></p>
<h2 class="ln-l2-text pb-4">Will we share information we collect?</h2>
<p>We may share information we collect with third parties as described in this below:</p>
<ul class="list-disc list-inside">
<li>Analytics: We may use third party analytics services,
such as Google Analytics, to grow our business,
to improve and develop our Services,
to monitor and analyze use of our Services,
to aid our technical administration, and
to increase the functionality and user-friendliness of our Services.
These services may collect and retain some information about you.
Google Analytics collects the IP address assigned to you on the date you use the Services,
but not your name or other personally identifying information.
Although Google Analytics plants a persistent cookie on your web browser to identify you as a unique user the next time
you use the Services, the cookie cannot be used by anyone but Google.
Google’s ability to use and share information collected by Google Analytics about your
use of the Services is restricted by the Google Analytics Terms of Use and the Google Privacy Policy.

<p>We provide you with the ability to access, rectify, port and erase your data. </p>
<p>When you delete your account, we permanently delete information you have provided and things you have created,
such as your email address and your podcast playlists,
and you won't be able to recover the data later.
To delete your account at any time, please login and visit the "ACCOUNT" tab on your Settings page at
https://www.listennotes.com/settings/</p>

<br><br></p>
<h2 class="ln-l2-text pb-4">How to remove my personal information from Listen Notes?</h2>
<p>Listen Notes shows information gathered from public RSS feeds across the web.
Even if we remove content from Listen Notes, it may still exist on the web.
This means someone might still find the content on the page that hosts it, through social media, on other search engines, or other ways.
This is why you may wish to contact the podcast producer and ask them to remove the content.</p>
<p>If the podcast producer has removed the information, it will eventually be removed from Listen Notes as part of our regular updating process.</p>
<p>If you are unable to have a podcast producer remove the content from the RSS feed,

const loadScriptsTimer = setTimeout(loadScripts, window.screen.width >= 768 ? 500 : 3 * 1000);
const userInteractionEvents = ['mouseover', 'keydown', 'touchstart', 'touchmove', 'wheel'];
userInteractionEvents.forEach(function (event) {
  window.addEventListener(event, triggerScriptLoader, { passive: !0 });
});

function triggerScriptLoader() {
  loadScripts();
  clearTimeout(loadScriptsTimer);
  userInteractionEvents.forEach(function (event) {
    window.removeEventListener(event, triggerScriptLoader, { passive: !0 });
  });
}

function _lazyLoadScript(loadType = 'async') {
  const dataLoadJs = `data-ln-${loadType}-load-js`;
  document.querySelectorAll(`script[${dataLoadJs}="1"]`)
    .forEach(function (elem) {
      if (loadType === 'defer') {
        elem.defer = 'defer';
        elem.async = false;
      } else if (loadType === 'async') {
        elem.async = true;
      }
      const srcLink = elem.getAttribute('data-src');
      elem.removeAttribute(dataLoadJs);
      elem.removeAttribute('data-src');
      elem.setAttribute('src', srcLink);
    });
}

function loadScripts() {
  _lazyLoadScript('async');
  _lazyLoadScript('defer');
