<h2>Information collected</h2>
<ul>
<li>When you register, Brevity only collects a valid email address that you control.</li>
<li>When you create summaries on Brevity, you do so of your own volition. Brevity does not require you to submit any personal data nor will it process the personal data that might be contained in your content.</li>
<li>Brevity does not directly collect payment information. Brevity uses Stripe (www.stripe.com) to process payments. <li>For delivering emails, Brevity uses Firebase (firebase.google.com). Your email address may be stored within their servers. Brevity has access to information regarding the delivery of those emails through Firebase.</li>
<li>For processing your text and generating summaries, Brevity uses APIs provided by OpenAI. As a result, this data is transmitted between Brevity and OpenAI during the process of creation. Further information about their privacy policy can be found on the OpenAI website.</li>
<li>Brevity sets cookies for authentication purposes as well as to enable essential functionalities of the platform. Brevity sets up a cookie to mark the user’s session ID, a cookie with the session signature to prevent tampering, and a cookie to tell the browser who the current user is. For analytics, Brevity uses Vercel (vercel.com), which does not use cookies and doesn’t collect any personal data.</li>
<li>For monitoring usage and maintaining service quality, we record the IP addresses of all visitors to our site. This allows us to limit free users to a maximum of three summaries per day, ensuring fair access for all users. This data is retained for a period of 24 hours.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Data Retention, Deletion, and Export</h2>

<li>All information collected will be retained only for as long as your account remains in existence.</li>
<li>The summaries you create are available publicly and are auto-deleted within 7 days of their creation.</li>
<li>Data stored on our servers is not encrypted, and is retrievable to the operators of Brevity. Brevity uses Firebase (firebase.google.com) for its databases.</li>
<li>If you would like to delete your entire account, please email <a href="mailto:contact@brevity.sh">contact@brevity.sh</a>. Your request will take up to 30 days.</li>
<li>If you would like to receive any other data related to your account, please write to <a href="mailto:contact@brevity.sh">contact@brevity.sh</a> requesting the same.
