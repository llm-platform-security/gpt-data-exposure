<h1>Imgflip Privacy Policy</h1>
<h2>Images you create</h2>
<p>Any content submitted to the Imgflip community may be shown to the public. For any images created with the
Imgflip image generators but not marked as "private", the image link will still be publicly accessible
meaning anyone who knows the link can access the image. Images not submitted to the
Imgflip community are unlikely to be publicly listed anywhere on Imgflip unless they receive
a high number of views, which are indicative of virality on a social network.
You can create images that are totally private by checking the "private" checkbox during creation,
but you will not be able to share links to these images.
If you are logged in, you can instantly delete any images you create.
If you are not logged in, images you create will be automatically deleted after a period of time if they
receive very little activity, or you can <a href="/request_removal">request their deletion</a>.
If you want to remain anonymous while logged in,
you can create images with "Anonymous" checked and your username will not be shown in association with the image.
</p>

you with more interesting ads. To learn more about behavioral advertising or to learn how to opt out of this type
of advertising, visit <a target="_blank" href="https://www.networkadvertising.org" rel="nofollow noopener">networkadvertising.org</a>.
</p>
