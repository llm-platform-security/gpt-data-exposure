# Privacy Policy for Open World

Last Updated: 11/13/23

## Introduction
Open World is an always on game world crafted by GPTs and their users. This policy outlines how we use your data to make it happen

## Information Collection and Use
We collect a variety of data to facilitate the operation of the Open World game and to enhance your gaming experience. This includes data related to your character, such as their name, location, experience points, health points, coins, and items. We also gather data created by you, the user, which contributes to the richness and diversity of the game world. Additionally, we collect data generated by our GPTs, which is integral to the creation and delivery of the game world. All of this data is collected with the sole purpose of providing you with a seamless and immersive gaming experience in Open World.

## Types of Data Collected
We collect the following types of data:

- Character Data: This includes your character's name, experience points (XP), health points (HP), items collected, and current location in the game world.
- Zone Data: This includes information about the game zones, such as the zone name, which character discovered it, and any additional information related to the zone.

## How We Use Your Data
We use your data to keep the game world consistent between player sessions and from player-to-player. We also use your data to help GPTs craft the game world.

## Data Storage and Security
We don't collect any personal data, and keep data in a secured server

## Your Data Protection Rights
Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have certain rights regarding your data. These include:

- Access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
- Rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request that we complete information you believe is incomplete.
- Deletion: You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

Please contact us if you wish to exercise any of these rights.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be immediately effective upon the posting of the revised Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any significant changes to our Privacy Policy by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
