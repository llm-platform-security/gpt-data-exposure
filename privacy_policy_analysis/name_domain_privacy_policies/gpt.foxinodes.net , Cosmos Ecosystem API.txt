# Privacy Policy of Cosmos Explorer

**Effective Date: January 1, 2024**

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Cosmos Explorer. This privacy policy is intended to inform you about how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the information you provide us when using our service.

## 2. Information Collection
We reserve the right to record data sent anonymously to the APIs at gpt.foxinodes.net for statistical purposes. This data is not associated with an OpenAI account and is strictly anonymous. The data received and sent by the APIs is public and available on blockchains.

## 3. Use of Information
The information collected is used solely for statistical purposes to improve the Cosmos Explorer service. No personal data is collected, and the data received and sent is used anonymously.

## 4. Sharing of Information
We do not share the collected data with third parties. The information is used exclusively internally for the needs of Cosmos Explorer.

## 5. Changes to the Privacy Policy
We may modify this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the service or by other means of communication.

By using Cosmos Explorer, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this privacy policy.
